ABOUT US

Gillego-Flores Dental Clinic

Gillego-Flores Dental Clinic began operations on July 15, 2020. Nevertheless, from 2004 to 2012, my clinic was managed by Centro Medico at Riverbanks Mall. Owing to Riverbanks' rising occupancy rates, our clinic opted to relocate to Benhel Building, a facility diagonally across Riverbanks along Andres Bonifacio Avenue, and was recognized as Clinica Marikina from 2012 until 2020.

OUR SERVICES

Oral Prophylaxis

Oral prophylaxis is a cleaning procedure performed to thoroughly clean the teeth.

Tooth Restoration

Tooth restoration focuses on repairing or replacing damaged or missing teeth.

Tooth Extraction

A tooth extraction is a procedure to remove a tooth from the gum socket.

Wisdom Tooth Removal

A surgical procedure to remove one or more wisdom teeth.
